Early Years

Matt Evens was born in May of 1985 to his parents Warren and Alana. He grew up in the small mid-western town of Grand Island, Nebraska. He attended school at a private school for much of his elementary education and home-schooled up until his sophomore year of highschool in which he enrolled at Platte Valley Academy where he finished his highschool education active in band, choir, and of course sports.

From an early age, Matt had taken a keen interest in sports. From being an avid fan of his states college team, the Huskers, to watching Michael Jordan on as he played for the Bulls. particularly in basketball, Matt played actively in sports during his homeschool and particularly excelled at basketball with his father as coach. The interest followed him into highschool and earned him the position of team captain by his junior year.

Matt played and toured with his academy team, the Eagles, across the mid-west throughout his highschool years. While Matt participated actively in most of the intramural sports, his passion was running plays on the court and ever improving his team's plays. When asked what his drive was on court, Matt simply stated that he was in a competition, but that the competition was with himself, to see if he could achieve higher than he had before. This philosophy stuck with him into college.

College and Career

Matt Evens attended Union College in the fall of 2004 pursuing a degree in teaching with adn emphasis in physical education and mathematics. Though he applied repeatedly to join the Union College basketball team, he was never selected.
